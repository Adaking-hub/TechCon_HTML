Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and has since grown into the largest tech conference in Africa. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and featured groundbreaking innovations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower, educate, and connect tech professionals across Africa by providing a platform for learning, networking, and showcasing innovation. We believe in fostering diversity, collaboration, and forward-thinking solutions.
